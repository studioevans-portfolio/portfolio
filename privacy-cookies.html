Privacy & Cookies Policy

Introduction

This Privacy & Cookies Policy explains how information is collected and used when you visit the Studio Evans website. By using this website, you acknowledge the information outlined in this policy.

Privacy Policy

This website does not require visitors to create an account, submit personal information or complete contact forms. The only information collected is anonymous website usage data through Google Analytics 4 (GA4). Google Analytics helps monitor website performance and understand how visitors use the site. The information collected may include:

    - Pages visited
    - Time spent on the website
    - Browser and device type
    - Approximate location (country or region)
    - How visitors arrived at the website (such as via a search engine, social media or direct visit)

This information is aggregated and is not used to identify individual visitors.

Cookie Policy

This website uses cookies provided by Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymous usage information. Analytics cookies are only stored if you choose to accept them through the website's cookie consent banner. You can withdraw your consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies or adjusting your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

This website uses Google Analytics 4, a web analytics service provided by Google. Information collected through Google Analytics is processed in accordance with Google's privacy practices.

Your Rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:

    - Know how your information is used.
    - Withdraw your consent to analytics cookies at any time.
    - Request further information about this Privacy & Cookies Policy.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in legal requirements or website functionality. Any updates will be published on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
