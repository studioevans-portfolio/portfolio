RSK Connect: Brand Identity

During my time at RSK Group, I developed the visual identity for RSK Connect, the organisation's internal communications platform powered by Workvivo. The project included branding, launch campaigns, employee engagement materials and digital assets used across the platform.

The RSK Connect logo was inspired by the double-arrow motif used throughout RSK Group's existing brand. Incorporating this familiar visual element helped establish a clear connection to the wider organisation while giving the platform its own identity. The arrows symbolise progress, connectivity and collaboration, reflecting the platform's role in bringing together employees across RSK Group's global network.
